The Sylvia Hotel’s Fall Foliage

While it isn’t a feat of architecture, nature’s handiwork has made this iconic hotel an aesthetic wonder. The sides of the building are covered with a lovely flourish of foliage, and it takes on exquisite orange, red, and yellow hues during the fall months. Passersby often stop to take snaps of the sweeping display, and the building makes multiple Instagram appearances during Autumn.
